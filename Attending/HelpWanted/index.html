Help Wanted

Help Wanted

Conventions don't run themselves! They rely on the dedication and hard work from people such as yourself. Do you think you have what it takes to join the NordicFuzzCon team? Do you want to help make NordicFuzzCon 2018 the best convention yet? Below you will find a list of open positions that we are currently looking to fill.

Singers and Musicians

We are looking for people who can either sing or make music, and who are capable of recording from home. As a convention we produce several videos every year, both for promotional purposes and for certain on-location events, and these videos often require music (and occasionally singing). If you would like to be creative, have fun, and make some phat beats and soaring crescendos, don't hesitate to get in touch!

To offer your services or if you have any questions, please send an e-mail to media@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line "Music for NFC2018".

Please include a link to some of your previous recordings and describe what sort of music you are able to record; electronic dance music, acoustic guitar strumming, vocals only, and so forth. Actually attending the convention is not a requirement for contributing music (but we do hope you will attend).

Fursuit Support

1 position is available

Work tasks will be to help refill the supplies and to help making sure the fursuit lounge is tidy and clean. Also to help setting up and taking down the fursuit lounge and other fursuit related areas.

If you would like to join or have any questions, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “fursuit support”.

Logistics, aka. Getting Stuff Done

4 positions are available.

We are looking for some hard-working Cheese Wranglers!

Work tasks will include helping to move NFC equipment from the convention storage to the hotel and then back again; helping out with on-site transportation; and assisting with some simple set-up. You will need to be able to be on-site on the morning before the convention the afternoon after the convention (late departure day).

If you would like to join, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “Logistics”.

Registration

3 positions are available.

Work tasks will include helping out at the registration table, temporarily baggage storage and more.

If you would like to join, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “Registration”.

Medic

1 positions are available.

Work tasks include helping attendees throughout the convention, working together with security much more.

If you would like to join, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “Medic”.

Photo & Video

2 positions are available.

The photo/video department are looking for two more awesome camera men. You will help recording on certain events and around in the hotel. The more material we get the better :)

If you would like to join, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “Photo/Video”.

Website content/graphics

1-2 position(s) is/are available.

The website team is responsible for producing and maintaining the website and forum. There are two different roles, the first will be to help with content and other website/forum related things. You need to know basic html and preferably some CSS for this position.

The second role is a graphics designer to help create website designs that are user friendly. Required is the ability to find and produce mockups of designs that can be implemented for all web sites.

If you would like to join, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “Website”.

What the Fuzz

3 positions are available

We need 1-2 more writers and 1 more on-location layout designer!

Writers join in documenting the con’s events and news. Work tasks will be joining in pre-convention planning of the issues, joining events and tours at the convention and documenting them afterwards, and helping out plan and organize the issues.

Layout designers will be helping out finalizing graphical issue layout before the editions are printed and distributed.

If you would like to join or have any questions, then please send an email to hr@nordicfuzzcon.org with the subject line “What The Fuzz”
