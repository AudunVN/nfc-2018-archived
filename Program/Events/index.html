Events

What makes a convention are the attendees. And we have a lot of attendees with a lot of different interests. So we want You to share what You can with all of us. Which also makes it so much easier for us to bring a great con to you.

This is where you come on, do you have something you would like to see, could you help make it happen. If you do not feel up to it, still let us know, we can help you help us to make it a reality. Many of our smaller events are made possible by attendees. If you feel the call, please let us know. Send your suggestions to events@nordicfuzzcon.org, tell us what you have in mind and what you could contribute to realize it.

If you have an idea let us know, you can back out if you do not feel up to it. But maybe someone else has picked up your ball and is running it for you. Here are some of the events we hope to be able to hold to pique your interest.

Other events

Eat

Afternoon Waffles
Wednesday Waffles is a cherished tradition for the Stockholm furries, but we do not limit ourselves to just opening day, EVERY afternoon is Waffle Weekday.
International Snack Exchange
We hope to bring back the traditional snack exchange, beware of hidden chilis.
Ice Cream Buffet
Of course we will try and reinstate this wonderful feast.

Show off

Strut your stuff
Come and show off what you have made and built, on stage.
Furries got talent
Show off what you can do, also on stage.
Fur-e-Oke
Sing for us, and you, and everyone.

Fursuit

Fursuit Games
Come join us if you will on our epic journey to find the hidden treasures of Eldorado. Or come and watch the spectacles unfold while our contestants are facing amusing challenges, and mystical puzzles that are aimed at being fun, silly and interesting for everyone.
Fursuit Charades
Fursuiters perform and the audience has to guess what or whom they try to resemble.
Fursuit Parade
Of course we will parade out fursuits through the venue, and for the public.
Fursuit Zoo
And also invite them over to see them in our zoo.

Art

Music Café
Here is your opportunity to show off your own things, a little lighter but still with a lot of skill.
Disco
Come shake your tail to pre recorded music, or maybe even live performances.
Storyteller’s circle
Bring your short stories and listen to what others brought.
Drawing corner
Open (almost) always, with pens and papers and friends to sit and draw alone or together.

Do

Excursion of Scandinavian culture
Before to convention we have an excursion out in Scandinavian culture.
Nerfgun War
Bring your own gun, or borrow one, “Cry Havoc! and let loose the dogs of war.”
Games
Try the arcade games, borrow and play a card of board game, or join any of the pen and paper role playing games going on.

And much more…

We need help from you

There are a lot of smaller events run by fans, check on the webpage and with conops what happens the different days. We are looking for more themed events, and need people that can spend an hour holding something. Here is a list of suggestions on what we would like to see.

  • The math that built El Dorado
  • Treasure hunt
  • Coffe/Chocolate/Rum/Corn/Wine tasting
  • Making of jewelry
  • How to make good furry convention videos
  • Songwriter's round table
  • Cards Against Humanity/Furries
  • Contemporary animal ethics
  • Anything My Little Pony
  • Whatever you can imagine
